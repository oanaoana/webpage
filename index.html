As a numerical analyst, I focus on algorithmic development for large-scale software. Following my PhD, I have been affiliated with several large-scale software projects. My approach is to identify numerical issues, i.e. efficiency and accuracy losses, in various applications and seek to develop methods that address these problems. The methods I have been working on were designed to be scalable and ultimately integrated into the software I am affiliated with or ported independently.
